About Me

Artist

Hi! My name is Syeda Safdar, a 21 year old Pakistani-American artist.

Art has been an integral part of my life for as long as I can remember. From doodling on the walls of the house as a child to declaring a major in Studio Art at William & Mary, my passion for creating has consistently remained with me throughout my life. Along with building my foundational skills, I currently experiment with artistic themes surrounding my interests and personal identity, aiming to develop my own unique style.

  • College of William & Mary | 2021 - Present
  • B.A. in Studio Art and B.A. in Computer Science
